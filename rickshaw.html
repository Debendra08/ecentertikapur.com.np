Shadab E-Rickshaw

Description


Shadab Electric Rickshaw is a reliable and eco-friendly passenger vehicle designed for strong performance, durability, and low operating cost. It comes in two models: Flexy and Duranto, both supporting Lithium battery technology for longer life and better efficiency.


Flexy Specifications

  • Battery: 48V 140ah
  • Motor: 1000W
  • Range: 60 to 80 km
  • Top Speed: 35 km/h
  • Capacity: 4 Passengers

Duranto Specifications

  • Battery: 48/60V 180ah
  • Motor: 1200W
  • Range: 80 to 100 km
  • Top Speed: 45 km/h
  • Capacity: 4 Passengers Max (5)
